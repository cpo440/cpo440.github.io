Introduction

I’m Rick Hoskins, an IT Support Specialist with a heart for service and a calling to help others — both through technology and in everyday life. With CompTIA A+ certification and hands-on experience solving real-world tech issues, I specialize in diagnosing problems, offering clear solutions, and supporting users with patience and professionalism. Whether it’s a home setup or a business environment, I enjoy restoring order and confidence when technology fails.

Before entering the IT field, I served in the U.S. Army from 1983 to 1992 as an infantry soldier, earning several honors including the Army Achievement Medal with Oak Leaf Cluster. I later continued my service as a police officer, where I developed strong communication skills, crisis management ability, and a commitment to doing what’s right — even under pressure. These experiences shaped the way I approach every job: with discipline, integrity, and a steady focus on helping people.

My educational background includes a high school diploma from Hamilton High School in Hamilton, Ohio, as well as studies in software engineering at Herzing University. This academic experience deepened my understanding of computer systems and software, while my CompTIA A+ certification provided practical, hands-on knowledge in troubleshooting, security, and support fundamentals. I continually seek to grow both professionally and personally to better serve those who rely on my skills.

My faith in Jesus Christ is the foundation of who I am and how I serve. I believe every interaction is an opportunity to show kindness, build trust, and reflect God’s love through my work. Whether I’m fixing a technical issue or guiding someone through a frustrating moment, I want people to walk away not just with a solution, but with the sense that they’ve been genuinely cared for. My goal is to serve with excellence, humility, and a heart that honors God in all I do.
